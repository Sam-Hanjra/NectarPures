A good scalp massage is not about speed. It is about **steady contact** and letting blood flow do the rest.

## Use pads, not nails

Work in small circles with your fingertips. If you feel tempted to scratch, switch to the softer side of a brush or add more oil.

## Time box it

Even five minutes counts. Set a timer so you are not watching the clock—just breathing.

## Rinse with intention

Follow with your usual shampoo, massaging the scalp again under the water to lift excess oil. Finish with conditioner from mid-length down.
