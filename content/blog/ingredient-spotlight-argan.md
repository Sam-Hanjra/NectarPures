Argan oil has earned its place in hair care for good reason. The kernels of the *Argania spinosa* tree yield a lipid profile that plays well with both **scalp skin** and **hair cuticles**.

## Cold-pressed matters

Heat-heavy extraction can strip away tocopherols and polyphenols. Cold-pressed oil keeps more of those supportive compounds intact—what you want in a finishing oil.

## Fatty acid balance

Argan is rich in oleic and linoleic acids, which help with slip and shine without the coating some silicones leave behind.

## On the label

Look for argan listed by its botanical name, or simply “argan kernel oil,” near the top of the list when it is the hero ingredient.
