Winter strips moisture from hair and scalp alike. The fix is not more product—it is **smarter layering**.

## Start with a dry mid-length treatment

Warm a few drops of argan oil between your palms and press into mid-lengths and ends. Avoid the root line on fine hair; coarser textures can bring the oil closer to the scalp on wash day only.

## Seal before you step outside

On days when the air is bitter cold, apply a light oil to dry ends after styling. This creates a flexible barrier against friction from scarves and coats.

## Wash day rhythm

If you use a richer scalp oil, schedule it the night before shampoo so your sulfate-free cleanser has less work to do. Your strands stay soft without buildup.
